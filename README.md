# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard (End-to-End Power BI Project)

## 🎯 Project Overview
An interactive, high-performance Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and optimize e-commerce sales, profits, and quantity metrics. This project transforms raw operational data into actionable strategic insights, helping businesses pinpoint high-performing regions and track annual growth trajectories.

## 💡 Key Business Insights Captured
- Total Revenue & Volume: Successfully tracked $534K in Total Sales with 49 Total Units ordered.
- Profitability: Generated $107K in Total Profit maintaining a healthy 20% Profit Margin.
- Regional Performance: Identified East Region ($31K) as the top profit contributor, closely followed by South ($27K) and West ($26K).
- Trend Analysis: Captured annual sales movement from 2025 ($330K) to 2026 ($204K) to monitor market shifts.
